About Us

We think that Little Pixies is a wonderful place for children to grow.

Creative

We encourage our kids to be Creative in their learning by providing them with the freedom to work in their own way and supporting them in the development of their interests.

Music

We make sure that Music isn't just an extra class but is part of the children's daily life. Our unique practice of combining the Montessori method and musical activities (based on Orff/Kodaly principles) distinguishes us from other preschools

Nature

We teach our children to take care of Nature through every-day eco-friendly practices, our natural playground and a fruit & vegetable garden that the kids help cultivate.

Philosophy

Little Pixies approach is the fundamental base for our teaching philosophy. It allows us to facilitate the development of our children's independence and their natural psychological progress, giving them freedom within a certain structure.
