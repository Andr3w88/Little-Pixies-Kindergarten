About Us

We think that Little Pixies is a wonderful place for children to grow.

Creative

Creative

We encourage our kids to be Creative in their learning by providing them with the freedom to work in their own way and supporting them in the development of their interests.

Music

Music

We make sure that Music isn't just an extra class but is part of the children's daily life. Our unique practice of combining the Little Pixies method and musical activities distinguishes us from other preschools

Nature

Nature

We teach our children to take care of Nature through every-day eco-friendly practices, our natural playground and a fruit & vegetable garden that the kids help cultivate.

Philosophy

Philosophy

Little Pixies approach is the fundamental base for our teaching philosophy. It allows us to facilitate the development of our children's independence and their natural psychological progress,.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Little Pixies where to entrust your child for so many hours in a day can be a difficult decision and we hope that the following references will help you as you choose a preschool for your child.

Parent Involvement

We maintain an "open door" policy. All parents are welcome to be at the school at all times. As a family-run kindergarten, we keenly feel the need to build a community for families, and we do our best to make everyone feel welcome and comfortable here.

Children at Play

When children feel good they are active, curious and ready to engage themselves in play.

When you come to visit us, make sure to watch the children already here. They are very happy and active and feel very much at home.

Celebrate Diversity

As an international preschool, we are able to expose our children to a wealth of different backgrounds, lifestyles and abilities. This diversity helps us teach them that there are "many right ways to live, many right ways to solve problems and many right ways to look at the world".
